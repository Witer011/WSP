About us

Jufeba Maschinenbau GmbH is a German company specializing in the design and manufacturing of advanced machinery and equipment for various industries. With decades of experience, we are known for delivering tailored engineering solutions—from small-scale custom machines to large, complex industrial systems.

Our strength lies in combining innovation with precision. We support our clients throughout the entire project lifecycle—from concept and development to implementation, maintenance, and repair. Whether it’s a unique one-off machine or a fully automated production system, we provide the technical expertise and customer-focused service to bring your ideas to life.

Our Products

  • Cutting Machines: Precision machines for slicing food products and other materials.
  • Custom-Built Machinery: Tailor-made solutions designed to meet specific customer requirements.
  • Automation Systems: Efficient systems for optimizing production processes through automation.
  • Bakery Equipment: Industry-leading deep fryers, chocolate tempering machines, cream cookers, pretzel baking machines, and more. Our full range can be found in our dedicated bakery catalog.

Our Services

  • Technical consulting and project management
  • Custom engineering and prototyping
  • Maintenance and repair services

Jufeba stands for precision, innovation, and trust. Explore how our engineering solutions can elevate your production processes.
